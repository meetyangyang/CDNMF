The quality and maturity stage of agricultural products is often associated with their color. For example, infresh produce markets such as red delicious apples and peaches,consumers prefer dark red fruit. Color is also used to determinefruit maturity and to detect surface blemishes and defects. In general, fruits such as tomatoes and dates are harvested before theyfully ripen. After harvesting, they continue to ripen and their colorchanges. For example, color is used to determine the length of timethe tomatoes can be transported and the type of drying processto ripen the dates. Most existing color grading systems are notuser-friendly and do not allow the operator to adjust grading parameters according to his/her color perception and color preference. The color mapping concept presented in this paper is easy toimplement and is well suited for commercial production.
