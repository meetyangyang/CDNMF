Customer order scheduling model has wide applications in industries where the entire order requires one shipment delivery. In many applications such as pigments dyes manufacturing, auto repair, grocery and so on, consideration of orders rather than individual jobs is often preferred. Moreover, the general machine environment considered in this study ensures that the methods proposed can be applied in most of the parallel machine situations that are customer order-oriented. Type splitting property is also a relatively recent field of inquiry and a natural property of manufacturing with continuous output.
