In collaborative product development,designers often need to collaborate on an overall design goal withindividual objectives and potential conflicts. Individual designrationales may not be completely transparent to each other. Thiswork proposes an automatic negotiation mechanism to conductdistributed design in such a circumstance. This mechanism isimplemented in a multiagent environment and tested with ascenario of distributed weight allocation in the development of atraining simulator. The test results validate the effectiveness ofthe mechanism.
