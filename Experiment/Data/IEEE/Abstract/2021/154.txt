The mean time between failures (MTBF) and mean time to repair (MTTR) of manufacturing equipment (e.g.;  machines) are used in every quantitative method for production systems performance analysis;  continuous improvement;  and design. Unfortunately;  the literature offers no methods for evaluating the smallest number of up- and downtime measurements necessary and sufficient to calculate reliable estimates of these equipment characteristics. This article is intended to provide such a method.
